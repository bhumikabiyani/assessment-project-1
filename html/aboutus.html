ABOUT US

MADHAV CREATIONS is a marketplace to "Discover Unique Indian Products" including Handmade, Vintage, Ethnic, Organic and Natural products from India.

India is a beautiful assortment of diversity reflected in our Clothing, Food, Lifestyle, Language, Music, Books etc. This diversity is so rich and diverse that an attempt to capture it on a single platform was never made. It is that sincere attempt by bunch of young insane guys and gals who think this is possible now with the Internet.

Madhav Creations uses a marketplace model to capture the regional variations of India. We connect local artisans & designers directly to global customers and thereby increase their livelihood, remove middlemen, help them create/promote their brand and thereby preserve our culture, traditions and values. We believe this journey will not only help artisans of India but also help customers discover and buy products which they otherwise are not able to do today.

As they say starting we was fate, reaching out to artisans was a choice, but falling in love with the idea of India was beyond our control. Lets relive that old India together again. we are funded by US based Investors: Nexus Venture Partners and Lightspeed Venture Partners. Madhav creations has also been hailed in the various media including Times of India, Hindu, Economic Times, WSJ etc as the "a HONEST Company in TEXTILES in India" which is bringing positive change in the livelihood of base level artisans and designers. Customer delight is our motto. Please write to us directly if you have any feedback, questions, concerns or suggestions.
